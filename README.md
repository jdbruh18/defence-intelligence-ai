Defence Intelligence AI

An AI-powered intelligence aggregation system designed to monitor and analyze developments in the defence, aerospace, and space sectors with a primary focus on India and awareness of global trends.

Lead Developers

- Jihad Haneefa (JD)
- Akshay Rajith

Developed in Collaboration With

Carbon to Cosmos — Space & Defense Team

Mission

To build an AI-powered intelligence platform that aggregates:

- Defence research developments
- Defence innovation programs
- Aerospace advancements
- Space technology updates
- Strategic global trends

The goal is to provide researchers, startups, and innovators with early awareness of emerging opportunities in the defence and space sectors.


Initial Capabilities

- Defence news aggregation
- Defence innovation opportunity tracking
- Space mission monitoring
- Defence exhibition alerts
- AI-powered summarization and insights


Project Roadmap

Phase 1 — Intelligence Engine
Phase 2 — Advanced Intelligence Dashboard
Phase 3 — Strategic Trend Detection
Phase 4 — Enterprise Intelligence Platform
